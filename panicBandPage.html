Panic! At The Disco

About The Band

Panic! At The Disco is an alternative emo band from Las Vegas, Nevada. They have had many members throughout the years, the only one the entire time has been Brendon Urie. He is currently the only official member.

Recommended Songs

Fun Fact

Between Pretty. Odd. and A Fever You Can't Sweat Out, the four Panic members went to a cabin and recorded an entire album on homemade instruments. It was called Cricket and Clover and told the love story of two animals. It was reported to have been lost, the demos, lyrics, everything. It is Panic's famous hidden album.
